Roll isi bayam keju/ Spinach and feta cheese roll

Puff pastry siap pakai adalah penyelamat dikala malas masak heboh. Roll ini bisa disajikan untuk cemilan ataupun makan malam. Dan untuk yang ngga suka keju feta (sebenarnya saya juga ngga terlalu suka tapi suami suka banget) bisa diganti dengan keju kesukaan masing-masing. Silakan mencoba! Nowadays I always have puff pastry or pizza dough in the fridge, just in case I don't feel like peeling onions and cooking on a pot or a pan. This meal can be served as snack or as a quick dinner. And for those who don't like feta cheese (I don't really like it either but hubby loves it) you can use whatever cheese you like. It is a no fuss fail proof recipe. Please give it a try!
